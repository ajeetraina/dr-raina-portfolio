About

{{ site.description }}

Dr. Raina is an accomplished Principal and Professor (Pharmacology) at Government Medical College, Haridwar. He serves on the Executive Council of the National Association of Pharmacology and Therapeutics and is a member of the Governing Body of State Medical Council, Uttarakhand. His expertise spans Pharmacology, Patient Safety, and Clinical Pharmacy.

As the Founder Coordinator of the Adverse Drug Monitoring Centre at GDMC Dehradun and Founder Member Secretary of Institutional Ethics Committees, Dr. Raina has made significant contributions to medical education and research. He was recognized as the Best Teacher at Government Doon Medical College, Dehradun in 2017.
